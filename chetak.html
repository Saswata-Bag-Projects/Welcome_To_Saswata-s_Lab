CHETAK

By Saswata Bag

Abstract

The project focuses on the development of cost-effective solutions to address safety challenges in coal mines. By integrating advanced sensors, communication systems, and real-time monitoring, the Coal Mine Safety and Monitoring Car ensures the safety and well-being of miners while maintaining economic viability.

Problem Statement

Coal mining is one of the most hazardous industries, exposing workers to harmful gases like methane (CH4), carbon dioxide (CO2), and hydrogen (H2). Additionally, poor visibility, fire risks, and health concerns such as low SpO2 and abnormal blood pressure make traditional monitoring methods extremely dangerous. This project introduces an automated system that eliminates the need for miners to enter hazardous zones.

Key Features

  • Gas Detection: Real-time monitoring of CH4, CO2, and H2 levels.
  • Fire Safety: Integrated sprinkler system for fire emergencies.
  • Health Monitoring: Tracks SpO2 and blood pressure remotely.
  • Communication: Two-way interaction between miners and the control room.
  • Night Vision Camera: Provides real-time video even in dark environments.

Technical Description

  • Gas Sensors (MQ-4, MQ-7, MQ-8): Detect methane, carbon dioxide, and hydrogen gases.
  • DHT11 Sensor: Monitors environmental temperature and humidity.
  • LCD Module (16x2): Displays real-time sensor data.
  • Buzzer: Alerts when gas levels exceed safety thresholds.
  • Arduino Microcontroller: Processes sensor data and manages system operations.
  • Fire Suppression System: Automatic sprinkler activation in case of fire detection.

Potential Market

The system is designed for coal mines, chemical factories, oil refineries, and other hazardous workplaces. With increasing global safety regulations, the demand for mine safety technology is expected to rise significantly.

Competitive Analysis

Compared to drone-based monitoring solutions, this system offers a cost-effective and practical alternative, ensuring real-time safety measures without requiring expensive aerial technology.

Future Scope

  • Integration of AI-based hazard detection.
  • Wireless SpO2 and blood pressure monitoring.
  • Enhanced communication system with cloud integration.

Achievements

This project has been awarded 1st prize in the Disaster Management category at the Rajya Stariya Bal Vaigyanik Pradarshani.
