EMG-Controlled Wheelchair

By Saswata Bag

Abstract

The project focuses on the development of an EMG-controlled wheelchair to assist individuals with mobility impairments. By integrating advanced sensors, motor control systems, and real-time monitoring, the EMG-Controlled Wheelchair ensures ease of movement and independence for users.

Problem Statement

Individuals with severe physical disabilities often struggle with traditional wheelchairs due to limited hand mobility. This project introduces a hands-free, muscle-signal-based wheelchair that eliminates the need for manual operation, offering an innovative and practical mobility solution.

Key Features

  • Hands-Free Control: Uses EMG signals to navigate the wheelchair.
  • Backup Control: Joystick for manual operation in emergencies.
  • Real-Time Feedback: LCD display provides live status updates.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Optional Bluetooth module for remote control.
  • Customizable Sensitivity: Adjustable EMG signal thresholds.

Components Used

  • EMG Sensor
  • Arduino Mega
  • Motor Driver (L298N)
  • 12V DC Motors
  • Rechargeable Battery
  • Joystick (for calibration and backup control)
  • LCD Display (16x4)
  • Bluetooth Module (HC-05) (optional for wireless control)

Working Principle

The EMG sensor captures electrical activity from muscles, which is processed by the Arduino Mega. Based on the signal intensity and predefined thresholds, the Arduino controls the motor driver to move the wheelchair accordingly.

Implementation

  1. EMG sensor detects muscle signals.
  2. Signals are amplified and filtered for noise reduction.
  3. Arduino processes the signals and maps them to movement commands.
  4. The motor driver controls the motors accordingly.
  5. An LCD displays real-time status updates.

Advantages

  • Increases independence for users
  • Non-invasive muscle signal detection
  • Customizable control settings
  • Cost-effective compared to commercial solutions

Challenges

  • Signal noise interference
  • Calibration for different muscle strengths
  • Battery efficiency optimization

Future Scope

  • Integration with AI for better signal processing
  • Wireless control through smartphone apps
  • Improved battery management system
  • Voice-controlled assistance
